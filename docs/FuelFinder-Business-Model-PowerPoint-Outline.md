## Slide 2: The Problem
**Title**
The Access Problem in Fuel and Charging

**On-slide bullets**
- Drivers travel without knowing station status
- Long queues waste time, fuel, and money
- Station operations are manual and unreliable
- Digital payment and access are not linked
- EV drivers face the same uncertainty

**Visual**
- Problem illustration or station queue photo

**Speaker note**
Explain how the issue is not just fuel availability, but lack of trusted real-time coordination.
